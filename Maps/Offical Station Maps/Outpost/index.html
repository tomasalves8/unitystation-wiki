Outpost¶

Outpost Station is the oldest, and most changed station in the game.

!!Headder|Header## |titleMining!! This is where the stations miners can prepare expeditions and offload ores.

Cargo¶

This is where the QM and Cargo Techs spend their time loading and offloading crates

Bar¶

This is where the bartender spends his time serving cold drinks.

Medical¶

Where you end up if you piss someone off or slip. Full of doctors.

Janitorial Closet¶

The janitors hideout, comes with a free Clean bot.

Micro-Chip Storage¶

Stores circuits and boards for the construction of machines and consoles

Lounge¶

A place for weary crew to rest or settle their differences in the ring

Office¶

An office, empty of purpose but full of boring potential.

Hydroponics¶

The home of the weed loving botanists, can also be called botany.

Eva¶

Stands for extra-vehicular-activity, but this is a station, not a vehicle. has all the essentials for a trip to the void.

Kitchen¶

The cooks very own barbecue joint, with attached slaughterhouse.

Gateway¶

A room with a portal that can lead you to some (generally very hazardous) special locations.

Dorm¶

A den of the most disgusting filth imaginable.

Command¶

A very large section of the station containing the rooms of both the captain and his best bud, along with the stations command deck.

Funny Room¶

A room containing essentials for both the mime and clown. HONK!

Artificial Intelligence Satellite¶

A super-reinforced fortress surrounded on all sides with he vacuum of space designed to house the AI.

Engineering¶

Where the stations power comes from and where the engineers spend their short breaks between parts of the station exploding.

Atmospheric¶

The stations Atmos Techs work tirelessly from here to hopefully keep the air all around the station breathable.

R&D¶

Where all the scientists gather and make half-life references between getting killed in various science advancing ways.[[File:R&D.PNG|none|thumb|308x308px]]

Security¶

Where the true dictatorial overlords of the station send their victims.

Church¶
